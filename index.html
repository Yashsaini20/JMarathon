JMarathon is an Accelerator program led by JM Financial, one of the pioneering financial conglomerates in the country.

JMarathon is created on the ethos to help founders accelerate their business through our market-validation and an opportunity to partner with us in our customer acquisition and engagement journey.


Scope for Investment for select firms that are found to be a fit in any of our businesses and also have the ability to scale up on their own

Transformation of the start-ups from Prototype level to MVP level, MVP level to GTM level and graduating the start-ups into full-fledged firm

How We Turn Potential Into Performance

Whatever you need to grow your startup, we’re here to help

MENTORSHIP

There are highly experienced and market leaders who guide the start-ups in their product, market fit and scale up strategies. Providing a multidisciplinary approach in developing innovative products based on identified business challenges.

SANDBOX

There are a host of APIs, of different products and enabling services which will be opened up for the start-ups to plug-in and validate their ideas in a real time and large scale environment to understand their market viability.

INVESTMENTS

JM as a group has one of the most established IB and PE practices in the industry with connects across the investing community. Validated and scalable ideas will always stand a chance to find suitable investors.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Workspace and Cloud Credits will be provided on a selective basis to collaborate with captive teams within the group.

ADOPTION / GTM

The group has businesses across the financial spectrum. The best validated ideas/firms get an opportunity to enter the markets with JM as a partner and to a world of new opportunities beyond as well.
